# Inventory
📦 Project Overview: Boutique Inventory Management System. The application is a lightweight, responsive Inventory Management Web Application tailored for boutique stores. It allows business owners to manage, track, and analyze their apparel stock seamlessly.  The system focuses on simplicity and data visibility, enabling users to add products, adjust stock numbers, assign pricing, and get a birds-eye view of their financial asset metrics in real time.

🎯 The Problems It Solves
Managing inventory manually or through disconnected spreadsheets often presents critical roadblocks for small-to-medium businesses. This application aims to eliminate those pain points:

Inaccurate Stock Levels: Human errors in spreadsheets often lead to phantom inventory or unexpected shortages. This app simplifies tracking by calculating total items automatically as products are added or adjusted.

Lack of Real-Time Valuation: Business owners often struggle to quickly ascertain the financial capital tied up in physical stock. The application continuously calculates the Total Inventory Value, giving instant insights into business assets.

Stock Outages & Critical Levels: Businesses frequently lose sales because they don't realize an item is out of stock until a customer requests it. The application features a dedicated "Products with Critical Stock" tracker to alert management when quantities fall below a safe threshold.

Slow Product Registration & Updates: Adding or updating products can be tedious. This system addresses this via an easy-to-use registration form and direct in-table row editing for lightning-fast updates.

🚀 Scaling the Solution to Any Business Model
While showcased for a boutique, this core architecture addresses universal supply-chain and warehouse problems across any industry:

E-Commerce & Retail: Can scale to track fulfillment statuses, multi-variant options (e.g., color, material), and sync with digital POS systems.

Manufacturing & Raw Materials: Tracks raw ingredients or components before assembly, allowing companies to understand cost-of-goods-sold (COGS) and prevent assembly line delays.

Food & Beverage (Restaurants): Monitors perishable items, flags critical shelf-life deadlines, and calculates active food costs to prevent wastage.

✨ Core Features Showcase (As seen in the demo)
Dynamic Metric Dashboard: Displays Total Stock Items, Critical Stock Alerts, and Total Estimated Inventory Value dynamically reflecting updates in the database.

Inline Table Editing: Users can double-click table rows to quickly update product fields (e.g., sizes, prices, or quantities) and save changes instantly.
